## **üìë Business Insights Summary**

**Key Findings:**

* Apparel drives most of the business revenue.
* Amazon fulfilment greatly improves order processing consistency.
* Metro cities dominate sales; South Indian cities show strongest purchasing behavior.
* Several Tier-2 cities show strong growth potential.

**Recommendations:**

* Increase inventory and marketing for top categories.
* Expand logistics and Prime coverage in key cities.
* Improve merchant fulfilment reliability.
* Launch customer loyalty and targeted promotional campaigns.
